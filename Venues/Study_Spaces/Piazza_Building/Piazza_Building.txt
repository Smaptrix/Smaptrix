Piazza Building

Address: Piazza Building, University of York, Campus East, Zone 9, York, YO10 5GW
Phone: 01904 320000

Description:
The brand new Piazza Building opened in January 2018 and is situated on the modern Campus East. The building hosts a lecture theatre with room for 305 delegates, as well as a large variety of state of the art seminar rooms and breakout spaces.

Opening Times: 
24 hours a day, 7 days a week
