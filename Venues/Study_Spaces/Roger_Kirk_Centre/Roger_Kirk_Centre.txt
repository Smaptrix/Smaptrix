Roger Kirk Centre

Address: Roger Kirk Centre, University Of York, University Road, Heslington, York, YO10 5DD
Phone: 01904 323112

Description:
Located in the middle of Campus West, the Roger Kirk Centre is a contemporary, large and airy venue which is part of James College. It holds a restaurant, bar and Costa that can be used separately or in a combination to provide an outstanding dining experience.
