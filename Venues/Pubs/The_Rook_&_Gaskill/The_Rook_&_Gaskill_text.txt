Description:
The Rook and Gaskill is an award winning pub located on Lawrence Street just outside the city walls. At The Rook and Gaskill we always offer a warm welcome with the best selection of cask ales, craft keg, canned beers, ciders, wine, and spirits. We also have a superb Burger menu (Served Daily)  as well as a delicious stone baked Pizza menu carefully prepared in our new kitchen. 
Dont miss out and make The Rook and Gaskill an essential part of your visit to the wonderful city of York.
