Description:
Charles XII is the closest pub to the University of York, located in Heslington.
It offers a vastly sized beer garden to the rear with car parking facilities available, whilst remaining traditional and cosy inside. We are also dog friendly.
Food served from 10:00am daily, and 12:00pm on Sunday for a freshly cooked roast.
Newest additions to the pub since a recent refurbishment include the installation of Sky & BT Sports, 6 hand pulled cask ales, 3 craft lagers and a brand new outdoor covered seating area for all year round use.
