York City Walls

Address: York City Walls, St Leonard's Place, York
Phone: 01904 551 551
Email: ycc@york.gov.uk

Description: Visit York's City Walls walkway, an elevated circular route around the city centre, which can be accessed on foot, for free, throughout the year. York's historic city walls are a major feature of our historic environment and civic identity. There are significant remains of all principal periods of their development; the city walls make visible over 2,000 years of urban change in York.
