York's Chocolate Story

Address: York’s Chocolate Story, King’s Square, York YO1 7LD
Phone: 019042 0527765

Description: York's Chocolate Story is an entertaining and informative guided tour through the history of York's most famous chocolate-making families and their finest creations. You'll discover chocolate's origins, how to make it, how to taste it like an expert and even the sustainable future of chocolate.
